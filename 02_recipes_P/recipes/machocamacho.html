Machocamacho

It is considered as an energetic dish for breakfast.

Macho Camacho is a dish that in recent years has become an emblem for the people of La Paz and is part of the mornings of many of them, delighting their palates with the mix of flavors and giving them energy for the whole day given its big size. Like a kind of sandwich in the company of a hot coffee, this element of the city's gastronomy is the favorite of diners.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of filleted beef
  • 2 medium red onions
  • 1 red bell pepper in julienne strips
  • 3 tomatoes in concasse
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 chili peppers in brunoise
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 fried sausage per person
  • 1 loaf of bread per person
  • 1 fried egg per person
